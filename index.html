About

About Us
We are an alumni–run esports association created for the Sydney Technical High School student community.

Established in 2014 as the Sydney Technical Counter-Strike League, we’ve since launched tournaments both independently, and in association with the SRC and with industry partners, such as Thermaltake ANZ.

Last seen at the 2016 Sydney Tech SRC Counter-Strike Championships by TteSports, which attracted over 160 student signups, and a crowd of over 300, we have now returned.
Pro League
The inaugural season of Pro League will run from late 2018, over a period of 10–14 weeks.

Eight teams compete in a two-stage tournament format. Each team will play six weeks of matches. The top four teams—2 from the senior group and 2 from the junior group—will qualify for the single elimination semifinals.

Fees
The entry fee for Pro League is $10 per person — alternatively $50 per registered team. Earlybird entry is $8pp for a limited time. Each game will come to approximately $0.83 to $1.67 per match, which we believe is quite fair.

These events are not-for-profit, however there are quite a few overhead costs including: production/design and server/equipment hire.

We would also like to have a budget to thank our volunteers (some dedicating 250 hours of their time over the year), as well as having a prize for our finalists. This is difficult without partnerships and sponsorships, which is what we are looking into for future seasons.
